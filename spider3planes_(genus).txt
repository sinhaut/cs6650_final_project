      <p><i><b>Planes</b></i> is a genus of crabs in the family <a href="/wiki/Grapsidae" title="Grapsidae">Grapsidae</a> that currently comprises three extant species:<sup id="cite_ref-Ng_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Ng-1">[1]</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/Planes_minutus" title="Planes minutus">Planes minutus</a></i> (Linnaeus, 1758), <i><a href="/w/index.php?title=Planes_marinus&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Planes marinus (page does not exist)">Planes marinus</a></i> Rathbun, 1914, and <i><a href="/w/index.php?title=Planes_major&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Planes major (page does not exist)">Planes major</a></i> (=<i>cyaneus</i>) (MacLeay, 1838). A further <a href="/wiki/Fossil" title="Fossil">fossil</a> species is known<sup id="cite_ref-Grave_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Grave-2">[2]</a></sup> from the <a href="/wiki/Middle_Miocene" title="Middle Miocene">Middle Miocene</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Caucasus" title="Caucasus">Caucasus</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> </p>
